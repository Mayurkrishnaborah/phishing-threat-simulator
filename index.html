📚 Learn: Spot the Scam

🔍 Check the Sender

Look at the email address, not just the name. Is it from your company's real domain? One wrong letter = scam.

⏰ Beware Urgency

"Act NOW or else!" is almost always a trick. Real companies give you time to respond.

🔗 Don't Click Links

Hover over links first. If the URL looks weird or doesn't match the company, don't click it.

💰 Never Pay to Win

If you have to pay money to claim a prize, it's 100% a scam. Real prizes are free.

🎁 No Gift Card Requests

No boss or company ever needs gift cards for business. Gift card request = scam, every time.

🏦 Banks Never Email for Passwords

Your bank will never ask for your password, PIN, or SSN via email. Call them directly if unsure.
